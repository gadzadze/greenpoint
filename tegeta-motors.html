About Tegeta Motors

Tegeta Motors is a premier EV service center in Georgia, boasting over 15 years of automotive expertise and 5 years specializing in electric vehicles. Our certified technicians, trained by leading EV manufacturers, use genuine parts and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to ensure your vehicle performs optimally.

Our facility features advanced charging stations, allowing you to charge your EV while we service it. We are committed to delivering exceptional service, transparency, and customer satisfaction, making Tegeta Motors the trusted choice for EV owners in Tbilisi.
