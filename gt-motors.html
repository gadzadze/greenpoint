About Tesla Service Georgia

Tesla Service Georgia is an official Tesla service center dedicated to providing top-tier maintenance and repair services for Tesla electric vehicles. Our factory-trained technicians use cutting-edge diagnostic tools and genuine Tesla parts to ensure your vehicle delivers peak performance.

Located in the heart of Tbilisi, we offer a comfortable waiting area, complimentary Wi-Fi, and fast-charging stations on-site. Our commitment to excellence and sustainability makes us the preferred choice for Tesla owners in Georgia.
